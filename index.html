Work Orders: Users can create, manage, and complete various work orders.

Inspection Schedules: Includes comprehensive inspection scheduling and tracking. Directory Search: Provides a directory search feature for easy access to information on all properties the user works in.

Offline Mode: Allows engineers to work in offline mode for tenant work orders, inspections, and equipment maintenance, ensuring functionality without internet connectivity.

Emergency Module: Equipped with an emergency module for immediate response and notifications for action.

Directory Search: Search feature for easy access to information on all properties the user works in.

Timeplay App

Over the years, TimePlay has become an immensely popular app in Toronto and Vancouver. I was part of the team working on this app from 2013 to 2018. TimePlay revolutionizes the viewing experience by transforming spectators into active participants. It enables users to engage as players, utilizing their smartphones as controllers, connecting instantly to a central screen for real-time multiplayer competitions. An indicator of its widespread popularity is the app's presence in the news even after its retirement. The news coverage serves as evidence of how impactful TimePlay was in Canada.
